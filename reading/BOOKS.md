# Software Design & Architecture Reading List

Status: Active

## Purpose

This document defines the recommended reading sequence for developing software design and architecture skills.

The objective is not to read as many books as possible.

The objective is to develop engineering judgment, design skills, and architectural thinking.

Books are grouped by priority:

* Must Read
* Recommended
* Advanced
* Reference

---

# Reading Rules

## Rule 1

Do not rush.

The goal is understanding, not completion.

## Rule 3

Never move to the next book without understanding the current one.

---

## Rule 4

Apply concepts to real projects whenever possible.

Examples:

* Hotel Management System
* Authentication Platform
* Learning Platform
* AI Agent Platform

---

## Rule 5

Focus on competencies, not chapters completed.

The objective is not:

"I finished the book."

The objective is:

"I can apply the concept."

# Expected Outcome

After completing the Must Read curriculum:

* Write cleaner code
* Design maintainable modules
* Understand software complexity
* Apply common design patterns
* Design application architectures
* Evaluate architectural tradeoffs

After completing the Recommended curriculum:

* Model complex domains
* Design scalable systems
* Understand distributed architectures

After completing the Advanced curriculum:

* Think and operate at a senior engineer or software architect level.
